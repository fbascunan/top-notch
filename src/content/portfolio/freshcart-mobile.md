## The problem

FreshCart was a successful web-based grocery delivery service looking to expand to mobile. Their customers were increasingly shopping on phones, but the mobile web experience wasn't cutting it — slow load times, clunky checkout, and no push notifications for delivery updates.

## Our approach

We built a cross-platform mobile app using React Native, delivering a native feel on both iOS and Android from a single codebase.

### Core features

- **Smart product search** with barcode scanning, voice input, and personalized suggestions based on order history
- **Real-time order tracking** with live driver location on a map, powered by Google Maps API and Firebase Realtime Database
- **One-tap reorder** for frequently purchased items and saved shopping lists
- **Seamless checkout** with Stripe integration supporting Apple Pay, Google Pay, and stored credit cards

### Technical highlights

- **Offline-first architecture** — browse products and build your cart even without connectivity, with automatic sync when back online
- **Push notifications** for order status updates, delivery windows, and personalized promotions
- **Performance optimized** — app launch in under 2 seconds, smooth scrolling through 10,000+ product catalogs

## The result

- **4.8-star rating** on both App Store and Google Play within the first quarter
- **40% increase** in order frequency from customers who switched to the mobile app
- **28% higher** average order value compared to the mobile web experience
- **60% of all orders** now come through the mobile app
