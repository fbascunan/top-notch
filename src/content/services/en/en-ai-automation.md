## Work smarter with AI

AI isn't just a buzzword — it's a practical tool that can transform how your business operates. We help you identify high-impact opportunities for AI and automation, then build and deploy solutions that deliver measurable results.

### What we deliver

- **Intelligent chatbots & assistants** — Custom AI agents that handle customer support, lead qualification, and internal Q&A using your own data.
- **Document processing** — Automate the extraction, classification, and routing of documents with computer vision and NLP.
- **Data pipelines** — End-to-end data infrastructure that collects, transforms, and surfaces insights for decision-making.
- **Process automation** — Identify repetitive workflows and automate them with custom integrations and AI-powered decision logic.

### Our approach

We start with a clear understanding of your current processes and pain points. Not everything needs AI — sometimes a well-designed integration or script is the better solution. We'll be honest about what makes sense.

When AI is the right fit, we prototype quickly, validate with real data, and iterate toward production. Every solution includes monitoring and feedback loops so performance improves over time.

### Technologies we use

OpenAI, Claude, LangChain, Python, FastAPI, AWS Lambda, Google Cloud AI, Pinecone, PostgreSQL, Apache Airflow.
