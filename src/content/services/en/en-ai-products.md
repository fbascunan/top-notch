## Intelligent software, not just automated

We build web applications that integrate language models (LLMs) as a core product component — not a chatbot bolted on at the end, but as the experience itself. AI tutors, study assistants, analysis tools with contextual understanding.

### What we deliver

- **AI tutors & assistants** — Educational apps with conversational tutors that adapt content to the user's level, generate exercises, and provide real-time feedback.
- **LLM integration in products** — Connection with OpenAI, Claude, MiniMax, or other model APIs, with prompt engineering optimized for each use case.
- **Supabase backend** — Authentication, PostgreSQL database, file storage, and edge functions — all on a managed platform.
- **Reactive interfaces** — Modern frontends with React and TypeScript, designed for fluid conversational interactions.

### Projects that prove it

- **[PAES O7](/en/projects/paes-o7)** — Practice platform for the PAES exam (university admission in Chile). AI tutor with MiniMax that explains solutions step by step, question bank, and progress tracking. React + TanStack Router + Supabase.

### Tech stack

React, TypeScript, TanStack Router, Supabase, OpenAI, Claude, MiniMax, Vite, PostgreSQL.
