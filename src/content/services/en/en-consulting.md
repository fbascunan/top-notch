## Expert guidance when you need it

Sometimes you don't need a full development team — you need the right advice. Our consulting services help you make better technology decisions, unblock stuck projects, and level up your engineering practices.

### What we deliver

- **Technical strategy** — Roadmap planning, technology selection, and architecture design aligned with your business goals and growth trajectory.
- **Architecture reviews** — Deep-dive assessments of your existing systems with actionable recommendations for scalability, security, and maintainability.
- **Team augmentation** — Embedded senior engineers who integrate with your team to accelerate delivery on critical projects.
- **Code audits** — Comprehensive reviews of code quality, security vulnerabilities, performance bottlenecks, and technical debt.

### Our approach

We embed ourselves in your context. We read the code, talk to your team, and understand the constraints before making recommendations. Our advice is practical — not theoretical frameworks, but specific, prioritized actions you can take.

For team augmentation, our engineers work as full members of your team. We follow your processes, participate in standups, and care about the outcome — not just the hours.

### Engagement models

- **Advisory retainer** — Ongoing access to senior technical leadership on a monthly basis.
- **Project-based** — Scoped engagement with clear deliverables and timeline.
- **Staff augmentation** — Engineers embedded in your team for weeks or months.
