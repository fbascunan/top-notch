## Build for the modern web

We create fast, accessible, and beautifully designed websites and web applications. Whether you need a marketing site that converts, a complex SaaS platform, or an internal tool that streamlines operations — we've got you covered.

### What we deliver

- **Static & marketing sites** — Lightning-fast sites built with modern frameworks like Astro and Next.js, optimized for SEO and performance.
- **Web applications** — Full-featured apps with authentication, real-time data, and rich interactivity using React, Vue, or Svelte.
- **E-commerce** — Custom storefronts with seamless checkout flows, inventory management, and payment integration.
- **Progressive Web Apps** — Installable, offline-capable apps that work across all devices.

### Our approach

We start with your business goals and work backwards to the technology. Every project begins with a discovery phase where we map user journeys, define success metrics, and choose the right stack for your needs.

We build iteratively — shipping working software early and often so you can validate assumptions and course-correct without wasting time or budget.

### Technologies we use

React, Next.js, Astro, Vue, Svelte, TypeScript, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Supabase, Tailwind CSS, Vercel, Cloudflare.
