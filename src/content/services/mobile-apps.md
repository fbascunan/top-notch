## Mobile experiences that users love

We build mobile applications that feel native, perform beautifully, and solve real problems. From consumer-facing apps to enterprise tools, we deliver polished experiences on both iOS and Android.

### What we deliver

- **Cross-platform apps** — One codebase, two platforms. We use React Native and Flutter to ship high-quality apps faster and at lower cost.
- **Native iOS & Android** — When you need maximum performance or platform-specific features, we build truly native applications.
- **App redesigns** — Modernize an existing app with a fresh UI, improved performance, and better user engagement.
- **Backend & API integration** — Robust backends that power your mobile experience with real-time sync, push notifications, and offline support.

### Our approach

Mobile development is different from web. We optimize for touch interactions, limited screen real estate, and varying network conditions. Every app we build is tested across a range of devices and OS versions.

We integrate analytics and crash reporting from day one so you understand how users interact with your app and can iterate with confidence.

### Technologies we use

React Native, Flutter, Swift, Kotlin, Firebase, Supabase, Stripe, RevenueCat, Expo, Fastlane.
